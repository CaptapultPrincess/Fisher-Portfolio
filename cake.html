Cake Bake

Cake Bake is a simple and short game where your only task is to bake a cake. After it's sucess, I kept adding on to the game. I made a lot of friends after Cake Bake, some of whom helped me create future projects. Despite this, I loved how small Cake Bake started, so I posted an original version. Cake Bake 2 is in production, and teasers for it are let out every once in a while.
